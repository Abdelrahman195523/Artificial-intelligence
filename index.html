Artificial intelligance defination:

Artificial intelligence is a branch of computer science, one of the cornerstones of the technology industry of the present era, and artificial intelligence - referred to as abbreviation (AI) - can be defined as the ability of digital machines and computers to perform certain tasks that simulate and resemble those of smart objects; Like the ability to think or learn from past experiences or other processes that require mental processes, AI also aims to access systems that are intelligent and behave in the way that humans behave in terms of learning and understanding, Such systems provide users with different services of education, guidance, interaction, etc.
