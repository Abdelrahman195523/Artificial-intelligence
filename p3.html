There are many practical applications for artificial intelligence, most notably those that come

1.Games:

AI systems are used in many e-games; they require strategic dimension and thinking, such as poker and chess.

2.Interact with the visual system:

Some applications of artificial intelligence can interpret and analyze the images that are entered into them; Such as facial recognition programs, image analysis for location, and other similar applications.

3.Interact with handwriting:

This is through handwritten font recognition applications, whether it is writing on paper or on the device screen itself.

4.Smart robots:

Robots do a lot of different work, because they can feel the surrounding factors like light, heat, sound, or motion through special sensors, and can learn from their previous experiences and make mistakes.

5.Interact with spoken voice:

Some AI systems can be used to listen to and understand speech, even if it is spoken with some noise or spoken in a language or in a street.

6.Provide advice and guidance:

Some applications of artificial intelligence can advise their human users in certain areas, such as the medical field, by analyzing symptoms of a disease to reach and treat the disease, for example.
